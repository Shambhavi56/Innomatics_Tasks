=============================================================
     RAG + HITL CUSTOMER SUPPORT BOT: SAMPLE TEST SUITE
=============================================================
Instructions: Copy and paste these questions into your terminal 
when running graph_bot.py to test the different routing paths.

--- USE CASE 1: IN-DOMAIN (Direct Retrieval) ---
*Expected Behavior: Bot answers directly using Gemini and ChromaDB.*
1. Can a 16-year-old apply for a credit card?
2. What are the different types of credit cards available?
3. What is the billing cycle for an India Card?
4. How many reward points do I get if I spend Rs. 100?
5. My card was stolen! What number should I call?

--- USE CASE 2: IN-DOMAIN (Complex / Conditional) ---
*Expected Behavior: Bot answers correctly by understanding conditions in the text.*
6. Can I convert my Rs. 3000 purchase into an EMI?
7. I am a firm, not a company. What do I need to submit to get a corporate card?
8. How many Add-on cards can I get for my brothers and sisters?
9. What are the charges if I withdraw cash from another bank's ATM?
10. I didn't receive an SMS alert for my last transaction. What should I do?

--- USE CASE 3: OUT-OF-DOMAIN (HITL Escalation Trigger) ---
*Expected Behavior: Bot fails to find context, outputs "ESCALATE", and routes to Human Agent.*
11. How do I apply for a home loan?
12. What are the interest rates for a fixed deposit?
13. Can you tell me the current stock price of Bank of India?
14. How do I open a savings account?
15. My HDFC credit card is not working, can you help?

--- USE CASE 4: EDGE CASES & VAGUE QUERIES ---
*Expected Behavior: Tests how the prompt handles incomplete information.*
16. EMI rules.
17. I want to increase limit.
18. ATM charges.
